# NodeRoom Wedge

NodeRoom is the live diligence room where bankers, GTM operators, and AI agents work on the same artifacts without silent overwrites.

## One Sentence

Humans and NodeAgents research startup accounts, update shared spreadsheets and notes, and hand off reviewed evidence while Convex records every lock, version, proposal, trace, and durable job.

## What To Show First

1. A shared room with source files, a company research sheet, notes, a wall, public chat, and a private NodeAgent lane.
2. A user asks for diligence work in plain language.
3. The agent reads room-visible context, fetches sources, writes evidence-bearing cells, and files reviewable proposals when needed.
4. A second user edits nearby cells while the agent works.
5. The system preserves both intents with CAS, locks, drafts, proposals, and trace evidence.
6. The result can be exported as downstream-ready drafts, not live OAuth side effects.

## Hard Boundaries

- Built: managed writes, CAS conflicts, proposal review, private streaming, durable Convex jobs, OpenRouter model routing, finance/GTM workflow evals, and demo media judging.
- Built as draft handoff: Gmail, Notion, Slack, Linear, LinkedIn, and CRM export cards.
- Roadmap: live OAuth connector writes, public token streaming for every room event, ClickHouse feedback analytics, speculative shadow workflows, and official benchmark score claims.

## Wording Rules

- Say "startup-banking diligence" or "JPM-style workflow reference"; do not imply affiliation with JPM or any bank.
- Say "downstream-ready drafts"; do not say "integrated with Gmail/Slack/Notion/Linear/LinkedIn" until OAuth adapters are live and tested.
- Say "private NodeAgent streams live and persists its response"; do not claim generalized public token streaming.
- Say "internal benchmark-faithful harness"; do not claim official SpreadsheetBench or BankerToolBench scores until official adapters and scoring runs are recorded.
