NodeRoom

Live Startup Diligence War Room

People and NodeAgents work in the same room: source files, research sheets, notes, wall posts, private lanes, trace receipts, and reviewed handoff drafts.

Why It Exists

Diligence work is fragmented across CRMs, spreadsheets, calls, source tabs, memos, Slack, email, and decks. NodeRoom makes the room the durable collaboration ledger.

Demo Flow

1. Intake

Import accounts. Existing rows update instead of duplicating.

2. Research

Concurrent agent lanes fetch sources and write evidence-bearing cells.

3. Review

Cell-local proposals and traces show what changed, why, and by whom.

4. Handoff

Draft cards prepare Gmail, Notion, Slack, Linear, LinkedIn, and CRM exports without side effects.

Proof Boundary

Built: CAS, locks, proposals, private streaming, durable jobs, OpenRouter routing, and internal GTM/finance evals. Roadmap: live OAuth connector writes, ClickHouse analytics, speculative shadow workflows, and official benchmark scores.
